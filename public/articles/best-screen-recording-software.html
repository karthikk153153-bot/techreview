🏆 Best Overall: OBS Studio

Free, powerful, and the industry standard for gamers and streamers. Supports unlimited scenes, plugins, and 4K recording. The learning curve is steeper but worth it for power users.

🎯 Best for Quick Tutorials: Loom

Simple, fast, and perfect for quick screen recordings. Great for customer support tutorials, product demos, and team communication. The cloud storage and sharing features are excellent.
